About Vellet Bakery

Our Story, Our Passion

Our Story

Vellet Bakery began as a passion project in a small home kitchen in Muscat, Oman. What started as baking treats for family and friends quickly grew into a beloved local bakery, dedicated to creating delicious, handcrafted baked goods.

Every cookie and brownie we create is made with premium ingredients and a whole lot of love. We believe that great baking comes from the heart.

Our Mission

At Vellet Bakery, our mission is to create exceptional baked goods that bring sweetness and happiness to our customers' lives. We are committed to using only the finest ingredients and providing a convenient online ordering experience.
